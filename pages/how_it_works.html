How BoKI works

Fine Tuning

Our model is fine-tuned with the BookCorpus dataset, a comprehensive collection comprising 74 million sentences retrieved from books. This extensive dataset enables the model to understand and generate high-quality literary content.

Embedded Model

The model is embedded in our server infrastructure, ensuring all computational tasks are handled server-side. This design choice offloads computational demands from the client, providing a seamless user experience.

Node Modeling

Node modeling allows for a detailed breakdown and analysis of book structures. This approach helps in understanding the hierarchical and semantic relationships within the text, aiding in more coherent and structured content creation.

Personalization per Node

Our platform offers personalization at the node level, allowing users to tailor specific sections or modules of their books. This feature ensures that each part of the book can be customized to meet unique preferences and requirements.

Re-writing Book

BoKI supports re-writing and editing capabilities, enabling users to refine and enhance their literary works. This functionality is particularly useful for iterative content development and continuous improvement of book drafts.
